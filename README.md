# SecureMojo — Digital Presence & Competitor Analysis

An internal intelligence dashboard summarizing target market, competitor positioning, SWOT analysis, and marketing strategies for **SecureMojo** — a pioneer in personal cyber protection. Designed for founder alignment, onboarding, and strategic planning.

---

## 🔗 Live Deployment
- **Interactive Live Site:** [https://jeswinjestin.github.io/securemojo-competitor-analysis/](https://jeswinjestin.github.io/securemojo-competitor-analysis/)

---

## 🎯 Strategic Overview

SecureMojo protects the **individual**, not just the device. Traditional antivirus tools protect system software, but 95% of breaches originate from human error, social engineering, and OSINT footprint exposure. SecureMojo builds a personal cyber protection layer for creators, founders, and professionals who have a high public profile.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Platform-by-Platform Content Strategy:** Fleshed-out playbooks for Instagram, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube, and Facebook & Threads.
- **Engagement Hook Formulas:** Core structures (PAS, BAB, POV) to convert cold traffic using shareable security hooks.
- **Core Content Pillars:** Educational and cultural frameworks like Hacker POV, Mojo Bytes, and Scam Education.
- **Follower Comparison Index:** Visual metrics mapping legacy identity/cybersecurity platforms (Norton, Proton, Nord) against Indian direct competitors.
